A day in the life of Bianca Negron

Hello there, my name is Bianca and I enjoy collecting ties and bow ties. Other interests include long walks in the park, reading, instagramming and spending time with family. I was born, raised and currently living in New York. I graduated from Queensborough Community College with an Associates Degree in New Media Technology and will be continuing my studies at the Academy of Art University for Web Design and New Media. School only taught me the basics of programming and design, so I decided to teach myself everything I needed to know to become a web developer.

I have always had a passion for art and design. From mischeviously finger painting on walls as a toddler to creating abstract images and designs in photoshop. I am always tweaking my web design skills, filling my head with new ideas for new designs and at the same time learning new techniques to perfect my craft and make these ideas come to life.

I am currently a Senior Digital Designer at Universal Music Group. I work with the creative team to design websites that include best UX/UI practices or design promotional content for artists. I also do freelance design and development projects for many clients around the world.

I specialize in designing creative, interactive, user and search engine friendly websites. I also create responsive websites, so the site can be easily viewed on any mobile device. All of the websites I've created have valid HTML and CSS code (validated with W3C validators), comply with W3C Accessibility Guidelines and are cross-browser tested. I make sure the projects I design are engaging, user-friendly, and aesthetically pleasing.
