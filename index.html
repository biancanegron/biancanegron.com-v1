A day in the life of Bianca Negron

My name is Bianca Negron, I am a designer & developer living in NYC. I became fascinated with the World Wide Web ever since my mom purchased Apple's Bondi Blue iMac computer(I had a tough time sharing with my sister). Later on, I graduated with a degree in New Media Technology. Helping clients create and build meaningful experiences is something I am passionate about. I currently work as a Senior Digital Designer with Universal Music Group designing websites or promotional content for artists.

When I am not glued to my computer I can be found playing my guitar, enjoying some good whiskey with friends, traveling, taking pictures of places/people/things, or attempting to play catch with my cat, Jade.
