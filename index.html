A day in the life of Bianca Negron

Bianca Negron enjoys collecting ties and bow ties. Other interests include long walks in the park, reading, instagramming and spending time with family. She was born, raised and currently living in New York City. Bianca currently works as a Senior Digital Designer at Universal Music Group designing websites or promotional content for artists. She also works on freelance projects for many clients around the world. Bianca specializes in designing and developing creative, user and search engine friendly websites. She makes sure the websites are responsive/mobile-friendly, engaging, user-friendly, and aesthetically pleasing.
