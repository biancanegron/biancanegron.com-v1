A day in the life of Bianca Negron

Bianca Negron is a designer & developer living in NYC. She became fascinated with the World Wide Web ever since her mom purchased Apple's Bondi Blue iMac computer (she had a tough time sharing with her sister). Later on, she graduated with a degree in New Media Technology. She currently works with Universal Music Group as a Senior Digital Designer. She is passionate about creating and building meaningful experiences. She strongly values thoughtful collaboration with clients and her teammates which helps shape the vision, connect with the target audience, understand and solve problems beyond surface-level thinking, and see the design through concept and implementation.

When Bianca is not glued to her computer she can be found playing guitar, enjoying some good whiskey with friends, traveling, taking pictures of places/people/things, or attempting to play catch with her cat, Jade.
